8/29/2017

Physical Exam Information

Sports physicals for the 2018-19 school year may be performed on or after APRIL 1st, 2018.  You can schedule an appointment with your family physician or at the school-based health center (651-2100).  Students must be registered at the school-based health center in order to have a physical done.  
Please complete this registration form before your appointment.

Student athletes participating in a Winter sport must provide a completed DIAA form  to the school nurse on or before NOVEMBER 1, 2018.   Winter sports include Wrestling, B&G Swimming, B&G Indoor Track, and B&G Basketball.  Avoid the last minute rush and get your physicals scheduled now.   

Completed DIAA Sports Physical deadlines are as follows: 
August 1st (Fall), November 1st (Winter), and February 15th (Spring).  Please call the Athletic Office @ 651-2727 x344 with any questions.
